## ⭐ Example (so you can see how) — "Animal Sound Race"

- **What you do:** The TV shows an animal. Race to tap the right SOUND on your phone!
- **Players:** up to 6, everyone at the same time, beat each other.
- **TV shows:** a big animal picture + the scoreboard. The TV says "Which one says MOO?"
- **Phone:** 4 pick-one buttons with sounds (Moo, Woof, Meow, Quack).
- **Turn:** 1) TV shows a cow. 2) Everyone taps fast. 3) First correct gets a point.
- **Win:** First to 5 points. Confetti and the TV shouts your name!
- **Fair for all:** Uses pictures and sounds, so you don't have to read.

Now make YOUR game even better than this one! 🚀
